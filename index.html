🎯 Who We Help

Many adults in the UK are eligible for up to £20,000 per year in student finance — and don’t even know it. We support learners aged 21–60 who haven’t completed a university degree. If you're working part-time, on benefits, or earning under £30,000, we can help you apply to university and change your future.

📋 Entry Requirements
  • Age: 21 to 60
  • B1 level English or above
  • British Citizen, Settled Status, or Leave to Remain
  • No formal qualifications required
  • Lived in the UK for 3+ years
🗂️ Documents Needed
  • Passport or ID
  • Share Code (if not British)
  • Proof of Address
  • CV (if available)
🤝 Our Support Includes:
  • One-to-one guidance from enquiry to enrolment
  • Help securing student finance
  • Ongoing academic support after you start
  • Multilingual support available
🛠️ How It Works
  1. Get in touch with us for a free consultation
  2. We help you explore suitable courses and universities
  3. Submit your documents and application with our guidance
  4. Apply for student finance – we support this step too
  5. Receive your offer and enrol in your chosen course
  6. Begin your studies with ongoing academic support from us

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I study if I am on benefits?
Yes. Being on benefits does not prevent you from studying or receiving student finance. Many of our students are in similar situations, and we help make sure the process is smooth and accessible.

Do I need to pay back student finance?
You only begin to repay your student finance once you earn over £30,000 per year. If your income is below that threshold, you don’t repay anything.

Can I study while working?
Yes! Our programmes are designed for adult learners. You can choose flexible study times — either 1 full day or 2 short sessions per week — to fit around your work schedule.

What if I haven’t studied in years?
That’s okay — many of our students are returning to education after a long break. We offer support from day one and throughout your course.

Do I need formal qualifications to apply?
No. We work with partner colleges that accept mature students with life or work experience — no A-levels or diplomas required.

What support do you offer?
We help you from start to finish: application, enrolment, student finance, and even with assignments after you begin your course.
